TIMING

My art work takes a bit to draw, and coloring takes around one or two hours, so I add that time estimate on to my main drawing time. A headshot colored will take around three hours to complete, and a full body colored will take around five or six for perfection...

I need you to understand it is my time I am using to give you the art you want, so I may have it vary depending on my personal life and other events. I also need you to understand this is not my main job, and I have relationships (smirks) and other important things that are more important than commissions. I will put as much time as I can for your art, and your art is the most important thing on my agenda when in work. I will put aside small things like going hanging with friends often to only when I have time. Thanks for understanding the time policy.
